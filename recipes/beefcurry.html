Beef Curry

Beef curry is a dish of beef in a spiced sauce or gravy, often made with a variety of spices, herbs, and vegetables

The savory sauce combines to perfection with tender chunks of beef and fluffy white rice, creating a truly irresistible flavor profile. Though the recipe does require a commitment of around 80 minutes from start to finish, the end result is well worth the time invested, making this dish a reliable choice when you want to treat your family to something special.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil divided
  • 2 1/2 pounds chuck roast cut into bites sized pieces
  • kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 large yellow onion diced
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • 2 tablespoons of ginger minced
  • 2 tablespoons curry powder
  • 28 ounces diced tomatoes
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a heavy bottomed pot until it just begins to smoke.
  2. Generously salt and pepper the chuck roast, then add the chuck roast and brown in batches. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. Add the onions and saut√© until translucent, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, and curry powder. Cook until fragrant, about 30 seconds.
  4. Add in the diced tomatoes and the lemon juice, scraping up the browned bits.
  5. Return the browned beef to the pan. Bring to a simmer and cook covered for 45 minutes. (If you are making with rice, now is the time to start your rice.)
  6. Serve over rice and enjoy!
